Welcome to BrainSmart 2.0

This is a game for 2 players, designed to test both your memory and concentration levels.

You will be shown 4 lists. Each list will populate individually and you will have 10 seconds to memorize all 7 items in the list.

After 40 seconds all the lists will have been shown.

A random generator will then ask you to recall the 7 items in ONE of the 4 lists.

The player who remembers the most items is the winner!

***TIP***
All items are listed in an alphabetical order: A - G!

Good Luck!
